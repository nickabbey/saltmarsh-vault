**Ehlonna** is the Greyhawk goddess of forests, woodlands, flora, fauna, and fertility — patron of all good folk who love the woods. **Neutral Good**, championing the beauty and goodness of unspoiled nature and action against threats to wild places. Holy symbol: a rampant unicorn (or a unicorn's horn). Worshipped by hunters, foresters, rangers, woodcutters, and others who live by the forest.

## Rivalry with [[Obad-Hai]]

Ehlonna and [[Obad-Hai]] both claim the woodland/nature sphere and are noted **rivals** — opposing philosophies rather than personal hatred. Ehlonna's good-aligned, protective, interventionist view of nature stands against Obad-Hai's True Neutral conviction that nature is brutal and should be left to its own balance. *(At this table the rivalry carries a personal edge: [[Viltevar]] worships Ehlonna while his companion [[Amos Crane|Amos]] serves [[Obad-Hai]].)*

## Ties at the table

- [[Viltevar]] is a worshipper of Ehlonna — his oaths and exclamations invoke her ("honest-to-Ehlonna").
