## Downtime, Day 1 (of the 14-day block before the sahuagin war)

We split between town errands and exploring the tower.

## The unexplored tunnel — [[Viltevar]], [[Shade]], [[Amos Crane]]

- [[Shade]] wanted to check out the **tunnel we'd left unexplored** (Door 1 from the lower levels). I went with him.
- We found a **vertical shaft with a ladder**, with **chunks of broken marble** near it.
- I cast **Detect Magic** and got an **abjuration aura**. I figured it wasn't likely to hurt him, so I told Shade he could take a closer look — probably safely.
- We strapped Shade to Amos with a rope, and he climbed the pair up to the top of ladder and into a large space between it and the door. Shade was able to reach the tumblers. 
- There was a **lock with tumblers**. Shade found it **harder than expected**; while he worked it, I thought about how magic can be used to augment locks.
- It finally clicked. I came away with **ideas for making tumblers more resistant to pushback.**

## The mausoleum

- We climbed up into a **mausoleum**.
- There was a **view of the sun**, and Shade worked out **which direction we were facing.**
- There was a single **crypt with a door**. The **floor was a dial inscribed with names**; the **center** was the opening we'd climbed through, and it **closed after a moment.**
- The dial **rotates one click to the left**. I **noted the name that opens it.**
- Shade pushed the crypt door open — we were in the **Saltmarsh cemetery.**
- The door **wouldn't open all the way**: there's a **chain on the far side** we couldn't remove from inside.
- I recognized it as a **smuggling route** and couldn't suppress a smile.

## The fight with Shade

- Amos asked what I was grinning about. I was evasive.
- Shade asked again, and I said I could see a **rich opportunity for a privateering side business.**
- Shade insisted it would **only ever be used as an exit**, and **gave me an order** to that effect.
- Being ordered set me off. I **insulted Shade's low birth/stature.**
- Shade **tripped me and jumped on my chest.**
- I **grabbed his ankle and twisted** as I rolled; he hopped free and landed; I got back to my feet and **drew my whip.**
- I **lashed him across the forehead with Booming Blade**, telling him he's **in no position to give me orders** and **doesn't speak for the Renegades.** Then I stepped back and **dared him to take an attack of opportunity.**
- [[Amos Crane|Amos]] stepped in to break us apart — and chided **me** about speaking for the Renegades, even though all I did was **answer a question.** (It was Shade who tried to order me *and* dictated an action for the whole group.)
- I **dropped it, opened the door with the dial, and left.**
- On the way back to the tower I ran into [[Vyth]] and **stopped to compose myself.**

**Timing:** The tunnel + mausoleum took about **an hour.**

## Downtime, Day 2 — contracts & the cove

A group of us stayed at the tower to **read the contracts.**

### [[Amos Crane|Amos]]'s egg hatches
- Amos took out his **[[Thousand Teeth the Devourer|Thousand Teeth]] egg** to check on it.
- In the salt air it **shook, rattled, and started to crack.** He ran off for **privacy to let it imprint on him** — gone a few hours.
- He told us afterward he **introduced the hatchling to Malaki** (his steed from **Find Steed**). They seem **fine together**, and Malaki is **weirdly OK being nanny to a baby crocodile monster.**
- Amos then went off to **training.**

### Cove planning — [[Crabber's Cove]] (2024 Bastion rules)
DM is running the cove as a **Bastion (2024 rules).** State:
- **Building 2** → **the Crab Shack.**
- **Building 3** → **undecided.**
- **The tunnels** → **menagerie.**
- Facilities that just need **staffing**: **arcane study**, **laboratory**, **menagerie.**
- Rooms that **need repairs**: **observatory** and **sanctuary.**

My take:
- I prefer the **observatory over the sanctuary**, based on the **charms each gives.**
- But I don't think **either is worth repairing/staffing yet** — I'm more focused on **restoring the defenses we disabled and destroyed.**

### Reviewing the bids
- I **reviewed contracts all day** as the guild members dropped them off, while we mulled over Building 3.
- **Morning bid:** A crew represented by a guy with a gold tooth who sounded a lot like Joe Pesci as Frank, the Wet Bandit from Home Alone. 
- **Afternoon bid:** a **dwarvish family** that would do **all the work through the Carpenter's Guild and not use the mining company.**
  - That gives them a leg up with me immediately — the mining company is tied to **[[Keoland History|Keoghland]]**, and any business I can deny them, I'm happy to.

### Magic market
- [[Vyth]] went to the **magical market**; we all gave him a **wishlist.**
- Lots of **amazing items we can't afford**, plus useful **commons and uncommons.**
- We might **each afford a rare.** Worth **visiting daily** — stock restocks a **randomly-rolled number of replaced items** as it sells.
- Vyth **shopped for the group** — most of us got something. **I picked up 4 spells.**: arcane lock, knock, mirror image, darkvision

### The last contract — [[Gellan Primewater|Primewater]]
- The **final contract** we looked at was **[[Gellan Primewater|Gellan Primewater]]** himself.
- I noticed **illusory text** in it, outlining **"creative accounting"** that would keep more of our coin in our coffers and **out of the kingdom's.**
- Primewater commented that he knew I'd **"see"** the benefit of working with him.
- I was **genuinely flattered.** I thanked him for his vision, told him it was **the best offer of the day**, and said I'd **personally lobby the Renegades** to give him the work — but that the **Renegades have to make the final call as a group.**

**Session ended here.**
