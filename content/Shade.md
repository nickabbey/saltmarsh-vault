Rogue multiclass and member of the Saltmarsh Renegades. Shade carries a ring of spell storing that allows him to cast spells with bonus actions.

## Notable Moments

While exploring the [[Lizardfolk Camp]], Shade knocked on a door and found himself face to face with six lizardfolk sharpening blades. He talked his way out of it with raised hands and charm, then ended up drinking with them. He shared a blade sharpening technique that impressed the group, earning their respect and an additional boon for the party. Their leader was [[Litrix]].
