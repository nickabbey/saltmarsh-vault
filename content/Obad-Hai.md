**Obad-Hai** ("the Shalm") is the Greyhawk god of nature, the wildlands, freedom, hunting, and wild beasts. **True Neutral** — nature as wild, brutal, and impartial, to be lived with in harmony rather than tamed. Holy symbol: a mask of oak leaves and acorns. He is the deity most commonly worshipped by druids, and is revered by many rangers and barbarians; his wandering clerics traditionally wear russet and carry staves.

## Rivalry with [[Ehlonna]]

Obad-Hai and [[Ehlonna]] both lay claim to the woodland/nature sphere and are long-noted **rivals** — a clash of philosophy, not open enmity. Obad-Hai's stance is the detached, hands-off one: nature is uncompromising and best left to find its own balance, against Ehlonna's protective, good-aligned interventionism. *(At this table the rivalry has a personal edge: [[Amos Crane|Amos]] serves Obad-Hai while [[Viltevar]] worships [[Ehlonna]].)*
