# Ferrin Kastilar

**Halfling druid, 140 years old.** Melancholy demeanor. Tends a shrine inside the **Grove of [[Obad-Hai]]**.

Travels with a bullfrog companion, **Lorys**, riding on his shoulder.

## First met
Session [[Session Notes 5-16-26|5-16-26]] — appeared when [[Shade]] started looking for the way out of the grove. *"Leaving so soon?"* Greeted the party with: *"I'm glad to have visitors to my grove."*

## What we know (after [[Session Notes 5-23-26|5-23-26]])

- **Follower of [[Obad-Hai]].** Runs the grove as a test site — newcomers get screened by a combat trial in which familiars/companions transform into hostile natural creatures. The grove resurrects on a healing pool; no one actually dies inside.
- Has the power to **end visitors at will** if he chose to. Welcoming so far.
- **Knew [[Udious]] and the tower.** Confirmed the Sea Grove druids helped Udious with daylight spells for his vampire-hunt. Sad to hear of his death; glad to learn he died with his mind restored and died a hero.
- Carries a deep well of regional history (140-year gnome — predates most current Saltmarsh politics).
- Holds **gifts to bestow:** magical leaf weapons + autumn cloaks (Obad-Hai-aligned gear) for dedicated servants; bulk **potion ingredients** (party left with rare-tier ingredients for 6 potions); knowledge of **how to unlock and use** unusual magical items like the [[Abracadabrus and Astromancy Archive]].
- Party is **welcome back any time.** Plans to return.
