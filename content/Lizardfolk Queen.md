**Queen Othokent** leads the lizardfolk of the Dunwater region. She is unusually worldly for a lizardfolk: in her youth she spent years exploring the marshes and secretly observing human caravans and villages, so she understands the outside world well. She respects those who treat her people as equals rather than as primitives to be mocked or patronized, and regards humans as potentially useful — if sometimes duplicitous — allies.

## What the [[Saltmarsh Renegades]] Know

- The [[Sahuagin]] drove the Queen and her people from their island homeland to the [[Lizardfolk Camp]] in the coastal marshes north of [[Saltmarsh]]. She is assembling a coalition (Cecaelia, Locathah, Koalinth, and [[Vyth]]'s former tribe) to retake it, and the party — sanctioned by the [[Saltmarsh Town Council]] — has joined the assault.

## Open Questions

- The Queen's specific agenda, how she weighs the party's conduct, and what she is ultimately prepared to offer or demand.
