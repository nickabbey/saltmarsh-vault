> **Status as of 2026-04-11:** a private long-term plan held by [[Viltevar]] alone. Not yet proposed to the rest of the [[Saltmarsh Renegades]]. Nothing below has occurred yet.

## Premise

Keoghland's crown sits distantly on the coastal towns of the region, levying and dictating while offering comparatively little protection in return. Viltevar views this as an oppressive arrangement on good people and intends, in time, to organize a smuggling operation that moves goods outside Keoghland's ledgers. He frames it to himself as noble resistance rather than crime, consistent with his knightly obligations.

## Planned Infrastructure

- **[[Renegades Headquarters]] at [[Crabber's Cove]]** — secluded bay, a cluster of buildings already off the town's attention, and a site that will formally belong to the party under the council grant once it is cleared.
- **The hidden passage beneath one of the cove's old cabins** — on first scouting it appeared to open into a second, concealed cove. Well suited to moving goods unseen.
- **[[Thousand Teeth the Devourer]]** — the juvenile the party retained. As it grows it will make a formidable guardian for whatever is stored at the site.

## Open Questions

- Who to buy from and who to sell to.
- What goods and what routes.
- What cover story keeps the [[Saltmarsh Town Council]] satisfied once the cove is officially claimed.
- How and when to bring the rest of the [[Saltmarsh Renegades]] into the plan, and which members are likely to agree.
