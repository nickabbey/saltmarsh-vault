The oldest tavern in [[Saltmarsh]] — a two-story building that rents sleeping quarters on its upper floor to the slow stream of travelers passing through town. It caters mainly to the dwarven miners and the town guard, and is owned by **Lankus Kurrid**, a retired officer of the Keoish army. It's also a known spot to seek an audience with [[Manistrad Copperlocks|Manistrad]] when she isn't working at the mine.

## What the [[Saltmarsh Renegades]] Know

- [[Amos Crane|Amos]] caroused here on the first downtime day ([[Session Notes 6-6-26|6-6-26]]), out-drinking the dwarves and town guard while scouting for an instructor in shield arts — a good fit, given the Goat's dwarven-miner and guard clientele.
