Death cleric and member of the Saltmarsh Renegades. Vyth has a complicated history; his former tribe is among the allied factions currently quartered at the [[Lizardfolk Camp]], which has created tension within the party.

## Background

Vyth's old tribe arrived at the lizardfolk lair as part of the coalition assembling to fight the [[Sahuagin]]. The reunion was not welcome. The party chose to avoid confrontation while under the [[Lizardfolk Queen]]'s roof.

## Recent Events

Vyth has been troubled by premonitions. Someone he owes a debt to is in danger, but the details remain unclear. The party suspects a connection to his former tribe, who were overheard mentioning a "trump card." A zone of truth session has been arranged to get answers.
