Gellan (NE male human noble) is a well-spoken, dapper older gentleman with a neatly trimmed beard and a fancy wardrobe. With his cunning instincts, he has positioned his family to become the most prominent merchants in town.

Because Gellan is the wealthiest man in town, he garners a great deal of popular support from the many feasts, entertainments, and other diversions he supports with expenditures and donations.

**_Personality Traits._** Wears the latest fashions. He is a patron of the arts and spends lavishly to support festivals, plays, and concerts.

**_Bond._** Gellan values his reputation above all else.

**_Flaw._** Greed colors every action Gellan takes.

**Smuggling**: Pro smuggling. Suspect that he's a leader in local smuggling. Seems to care that innocents don't get hurt.

**Faction / council.** Head of a prominent merchant family with textile and lumber export interests; holds a seat on the [[Saltmarsh Town Council|town council]] among the [[Traditionalists|Traditionalist]] faction, while publicly playing the foppish dandy who cares little for council drudgery.

## What the [[Saltmarsh Renegades]] Know

- In the [[Session Notes 6-6-26|6-6-26]] downtime he bid on the [[Crabber's Cove]] tower repairs — with a "creative accounting" arrangement (to keep coin out of the crown's reach) woven into the contract in **illusory text** that [[Viltevar]] alone could read. Viltevar was flattered, called it the best offer of the day, and pledged to lobby the Renegades on his behalf.
