Party member of the Saltmarsh Renegades. Has a contact relationship with [[Gellan Primewater]], who provides the group with missions. Knows the Mending spell, which he used to repair the party's boat after the [[Sahuagin]] attack on the voyage north.

## Notable Moments

Fell into the water during the sahuagin ambush along the coast. Viltevar threw him a rope and braced to haul him back aboard. Once recovered, he mended the damaged ship with gathered wood and the Mending cantrip, getting the party back on the water within an hour.

## Departure from the Renegades

Stepped away from the [[Saltmarsh Renegades]] after [[Haldrin Amberale]] announced he was staying in the city to build a temple. Flint offered to remain behind as well, lending his skills to the town guard in rebuilding [[Saltmarsh]]'s defenses.
