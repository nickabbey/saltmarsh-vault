
Sauriv is extremely wise, and it is due to his inspiration that the strategy of forging an alliance against the sahuagin was devised.  Reports to [[Lizardfolk Queen]]. He arranged for the purchase of the weapons the Sea Ghost has been transporting. His appearance betrays his age. His teeth and claws are stained and blunted, his eyesight is fading, and his senses of hearing and smell have lost their acuity. He wears a large gold chain around his neck. Meeting that Sauriv has with Vyth and Shade informs the party of details of the Lizardfolk tribes war plans against the dangerous sahuagin lair.

1. They plan to contact the Saltmarsh council in 5 days to strategize. In 10-15 days they plan to make their first move with the parties help.
2. The war army with include help from the following tribes: Cecaelia tribe, Koalinth tribe, Locathah tribe, Kaegro Lizardfolk tribe, and the Otho Lizardfolk tribe acting as the main force and overseers of operations.
3. Different tribes will send a fighter of there own to help your party when the time comes to compete your mission for the war.
