Relative to a Vineyard that I know and have heard of
Paladin guide from the northern Dreadwood

**Faith.** A devoted servant of [[Obad-Hai]], the god of wild nature. At the [[Grove of Obad-Hai]] within the [[Crabber's Cove]] tower, the grove's druid [[Ferrin Kastilar]] affirmed Amos's calling and gifted him Obad-Hai-blessed gear (a leaf-shaped sword and an autumn cloak). His companion [[Viltevar]] worships the rival woodland goddess [[Ehlonna]] — a standing, good-natured theological needle between them.
