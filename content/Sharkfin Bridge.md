2A single large bridge spans the river, with shops and homes along its length. The bridge predates the village and is large enough for laden carts to pass two abreast.

## What the Renegades Found

Crossing the bridge on their return from the west on 2026-04-11, [[Viltevar]] noticed a cold shiver running down his spine. The sensation worsened to active nausea on his second crossing. He asked whether anyone else had felt it. [[Kaldor]], who is half-elven, admitted that he felt it every time he set foot on the bridge and had grown so used to it that he almost no longer noticed.

Halting the party mid-span, Viltevar cast Detect Magic and found a necromancy curse woven into the stonework itself. He cannot lift curses; [[Vyth]] may yet be able to. [[Shade]] added a further observation: the stone the bridge is built from is not native to this region. Whatever was written into it was imported on purpose.

Just as the party was cataloguing the aura, [[Vyth]] noticed something else on the bridge he could not immediately name. The matter was still being examined when the council's summons pulled them onward.

## Open Questions

- What [[Vyth]] noticed on the bridge that he could not put a name to.
- Where the non-native stone originated and who brought it here.
- Whether the curse can be lifted, and what purpose it was originally laid for.
