The [[Saltmarsh Town Council]] has granted the [[Saltmarsh Renegades]] the right to claim a parcel of vacant land within [[Saltmarsh]] in recognition of services rendered. The party has chosen [[Crabber's Cove]] as the site.

The cove is not yet theirs to occupy; it must first be cleared of its current hazards, and a full accounting made of what lies beyond the hidden passage under one of the old cabins.

## Why the Cove

- A secluded bay, physically set apart from the rest of town.
- A cluster of weathered buildings large enough to quarter a small company.
- A three-story observatory tower that could be restored to working order.
- A concealed passage beneath one of the old cabins that on first scouting appeared to open into a second, hidden cove. The full extent is still unknown.
