Aquatic raiders and the primary antagonist faction in the region. The sahuagin drove the [[Lizardfolk Queen]] and her people from their island homeland, forcing them to resettle at the [[Lizardfolk Camp]] in the coastal marshes north of [[Saltmarsh]].

## Threat

The sahuagin are aggressive and territorial. They ambushed the party with blade masters during the sea voyage to the lizardfolk lair, nearly destroying the boat.

## Coalition Against Them

The [[Lizardfolk Queen]] is assembling a coalition of races to retake her homeland. Allied factions quartered at the lair include Cecaelia, Locathah, Koalinth, and [[Vyth]]'s former lizardfolk tribe. The party has been sanctioned by the [[Saltmarsh Town Council]] to join the assault.

[[Thousand Teeth the Devourer]] was also recruited by the party to migrate to the coast and hunt sahuagin.
