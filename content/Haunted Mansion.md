A derelict mansion on the coast near [[Saltmarsh]], used as a front for a smuggling operation until the [[Saltmarsh Renegades]] cleared it out. The [[Lizardfolk Camp]] sits roughly ten miles along the coast to the west.

## What the Renegades Found

SMR was tipped off about the mansion by [[Eda Oweland]] and [[Gellan Primewater]] and discovered a smuggling crew working out of it, moving a shipment of weapons. Young lizardfolk were being held on-site as labor, sent by their tribe to make sure the weapons actually reached their camp. The mansion's resident was killed in the fight; his corpse was later animated.

The party wiped out the smugglers and freed the young lizardfolk. The survivors explained why they were there and asked SMR to finish the delivery the raid had interrupted.

## Significance to the Campaign

This encounter is the origin of the lizardfolk weapon-delivery quest that shaped everything that followed. The young lizardfolk became the party's guides, which led to the audience with the [[Lizardfolk Queen]], the alliance against the [[Sahuagin]], and now the war council under [[Sauriv (Lizrdfolk Warchief)|Sauriv]]. The weapons being staged for return to the lizardfolk are the same cargo the smugglers had been transporting.
