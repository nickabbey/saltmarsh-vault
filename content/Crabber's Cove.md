A cluster of weathered, abandoned buildings along the shore of a secluded bay just east of the [[Saltmarsh]] docks. The residents gave up on the cove years ago, and thousands of crabs have since taken over the crumbling cabins. Crabbers from town still work the edges, but most avoid the interior; more than one overeager fisher has walked into the clacking darkness and not come back.

The [[Saltmarsh Renegades]] have claimed the site as their future [[Renegades Headquarters|home base]] under a council grant, pending the clearing of its current hazards.

## Layout

The site is four buildings plus a hidden basement reached through a stone fireplace.

**Building 1** — A two-story structure roughly 100 ft long. Ground floor 60 ft wide, second floor 40 ft wide.

**Building 2** — Twin of Building 3, without the vampire's chamber.

**Building 3** — Twin of Building 2, with one addition: the chamber in which a vampire is trapped.

**Building 4 — Observatory Tower** (three stories, 60 ft radius):

- *Ground floor:* A half-circle entry space 30 ft deep. A door in the right corner opens into a study. A staircase at the far right climbs to the second floor.
- *Second floor:* Turning right off the stairs, a 5 ft hallway runs across the tower with four doors, two rooms on each side. The first and second rooms on the left connect to each other through a door locked on both sides. Both rooms on the right open into the same space, an old chapel to [[Procan]]. At the end of the hallway on the left, a staircase climbs to a locked door (DC 15) on the third floor.
- *Third floor:* A large open chamber set up as an old star-charting laboratory. Gears and levers suggest the roof could be opened with some time and repairs. A telescope, charting maps, and books remain. A ladder rises to a loft ringing the inside of a 25 ft domed roof.

### Secret Basement (under a stone fireplace)

A six-sided chamber with five locked steel doors and one unlocked door. Three of the five locked doors carry magical wards: **one necromancy, two abjuration.** On first scouting, the space past the fireplace passage gave the impression of opening into another hidden cove.

## What the Renegades Know

[[Saltmarsh Renegades|SMR]] have scouted the site, freed a number of human captives being held there, and confirmed that a vampire is trapped on the grounds, bound by a curse: he can only leave if someone pure of heart carries him out. They withdrew after scouting and are planning to return to deal with him.

## Open Questions

- Who the freed humans were, whether they gave any information about their captors, and whether any of their stories connect to known factions in [[Saltmarsh]].
- What lies behind each of the five locked steel doors in the hexagonal basement, and which doors carry the necromancy ward versus the two abjuration wards.
- The vampire's name, history, and what the party intends to do with him given the carry-out curse.
