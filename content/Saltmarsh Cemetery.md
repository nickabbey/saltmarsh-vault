The town cemetery of [[Saltmarsh]] — well-kept, though many of its "graves" are only memorial stones for those lost at sea. Its gravedigger, **Krag**, is a half-orc and an informal town historian/loremaster: he researches the folk buried here and regional events, helps organize and translate [[Eliander Fireborn|Eliander]]'s library, and is a valuable source of local lore for those willing to help his research.

## What the [[Saltmarsh Renegades]] Know

- [[Viltevar]] and [[Shade]] emerged here through the **hidden crypt-dial passage** at the end of the [[Crabber's Cove]] smuggling tunnel ([[Session Notes 6-6-26|6-6-26]]) — a name-keyed crypt door, chained shut from the cemetery side. Viltevar clocked it instantly as a privateering opportunity.
- The party has not yet met or spoken with Krag.
