## Tea with Ferrin

- **[[Ferrin Kastilar|Ferrin]]** was welcoming. Chatted with **[[Amos Crane|Amos]]** as he led the party into a tranquil clearing with a pond at its center.
- Graciously prepared **tea** for the party. Genuine hospitality, no apparent edge.
- Once everyone had relaxed and drunk, Ferrin asked Amos about **the tenets of his oath**.
- Amos answered with his usual refrain — roughly: *"Let nature take its course. Sometimes that means violence."*

## Something tips over

Unclear what triggered it — wrong answer, a test of worthiness from [[Obad-Hai]] or the grove itself, or something else. But the mood broke.

- **Every familiar and awakened companion in the party morphed into a natural-ish creature.**
  - [[Viltevar]]'s **awakened spellbook → giant spider**, adjacent to Viltevar.
  - [[Viltevar]]'s **weasel familiar → sabretoothed tiger**, also adjacent to Viltevar.
  - Other party familiars → **giant constrictor snake**, **mammoth**.
- **[[Kaldor]]**, who was lazing in the pond, **morphed into a giant pufferfish**.

## The fight

### Viltevar misreads the moment

- Didn't immediately register the new creatures as hostile. Attempted an **animal handling check to hop on the sabretoothed tiger** (visions of riding it regally into the fray).
- Check failed. Tiger was decidedly not a mount.
- **Misty Step** clear of the tiger and spider. Tried to move further away — **the forest itself closed in**, blocking further retreat.
- Dodged, expecting the worst.

### The owlbear arrives

- **An owlbear came crashing down** on Viltevar. (Not from the familiar-morph list — fresh addition from the grove itself.)
- **Viltevar reaction: Shield**. Spell deflected razor claws an instant before they would have taken his head off.

### The rest of the party

- **[[Shade]]** went hostile, was **crowd-controlled fast** — **restrained and grappled by the giant constrictor**.
- **[[Amos Crane|Amos]]** charged in. **Critted the giant spider.**
- **[[Viltevar]] felt nothing** — the connection to the **arcane mind in his spellbook severed**. (Spider/book down? Bond broken either way.)
- **[[Kaldor]]** (pufferfish) **expanded two size categories** — now filling a **15-ft square on each side**.

**Note:** At the moment the transformations triggered, **[[Ferrin Kastilar|Ferrin]] himself faded away** along with **Lorys**. Gone from the field for the duration of the fight.

### Round 2 — it gets worse

- **[[Shade]]** broke free of the constrictor at the top of the round.
- **[[Kaldor]]** transformed again — now some new monstrosity with a **20-foot neck**.
- Kaldor ran past [[Viltevar]] and notably **made it further than Viltevar did before the trees closed in** (different boundary per creature? or Kaldor specifically getting more leeway?).
- **Long-necked Kaldor** struck **[[Amos Crane|Amos]]** from 20 feet away — heavy damage.
- **[[Viltevar]]** circled around the owlbear and dropped a **Fireball** on the mammoth and the constrictor.
- **Mammoth** charged Viltevar, gored him, knocked him prone. Reared back to stomp — somehow missed.
- **[[Vyth]]** (still restrained) swung at Kaldor, missed badly. Cast **Spiritual Weapon**, landed solid force damage.
- **[[Amos Crane|Amos]]** went after Viltevar's book/spider.

### Round 3 — Viltevar drops

- **[[Kaldor]]** transformed *again* — now a **huge crab**.
- During the transition **[[Amos Crane|Amos]] reacted to squirm free** and landed on the ground.
- **[[Shade]]** tried to goad the mammoth into attacking him — didn't take the bait — but dealt a ton of damage and moved away still taunting.
- **[[Viltevar]]** stood, moved south without provoking an OA, **Fireballed** between the owlbear and the mammoth. **Mammoth should have died but fully rejuvenated.** (Confirmed test-arena mechanic — nothing in here actually dies.)
- **[[Kaldor]]** shrieked to frighten the party — everyone saved.
- **Owlbear** clawed [[Viltevar]] — **Silvery Barbs** burned to soften it; Viltevar gave himself advantage hoping it would matter. Left at **1 HP**.
- **Mammoth** gored [[Viltevar]] for **23 over** his remaining HP. **Viltevar goes down.**
- (Told after the fact:) **[[Vyth]], out of spells, made a desperate weapon attack on Kaldor** in his giant form.

## Aftermath — it was a test

- [[Viltevar]] comes to at **1 HP**.
- **[[Ferrin Kastilar|Ferrin]] strolls back out:** *"Wasn't that fun?!"*
- [[Amos Crane|Amos]] carries [[Viltevar]] into the **pool** at Ferrin's prompting — both regain HP rapidly in the water.
- [[Viltevar]] **feels the spellbook's mind again** — bond restored.
- Ferrin explains: *"This was a test. The followers of [[Obad-Hai]] in this group have potential. I needed to draw it out."* Claims they were never in danger.
  - Implied: he could end the party at will. [[Viltevar]] keeps the "didn't feel un-dangerous" quip to himself.

## Ferrin opens the orb — *Abracadabrus and Astromancy Archive*

- Ferrin shows [[Viltevar]] **how to open** the astral sphere item recovered last session.
- [[Viltevar]] now perceives a **heads-up display in his mind** — spells aligned with **celestial spheres**.
- **The item** is the canonical **Astromancy Archive** (Tasha's Cauldron of Everything) with a homebrew skin and an embedded journal — see [[Abracadabrus and Astromancy Archive]] for full mechanics.
  - Divination spell list (Augury, Divination, Find the Path, Foresight, Locate Creature, Locate Object), functions as a spellbook + spellcasting focus.
  - **3 charges**, regains 1d3/dawn. Two uses: swap a prepared spell for a divination spell from the archive (1 charge, 1 min), or a d4 reaction bonus/penalty on a visible creature's roll within 30 ft (1 charge).
  - Embedded **journal from [[Udious]]** is the homebrew layer.

### The journal — Udious's story

> *"If you have found and unlocked this, you are either of great intelligence, or a trusted friend. I leave you the tower and information in it."*

- Signed **"Udious Odiuous"** — full name of [[Udious]] established.
- After their adventuring days, Udious and his party **lived in this tower**.
- They found the **tomb of [[Xoltec]]**, a vampire.
- **Xoltec came from across the sea.**
- Xoltec was **mind-controlling local priests** — one of whom was a friend of the party.
- They fought. **One companion died. One priest escaped.**
- The **escaped priest stole a different companion's weapon.**
- Udious spent the rest of his life trying to end Xoltec.
- Learned much. **Sold another weapon to [[Keoland History|Keoghland]]** to fund the tower's construction. Left with no resources to finish the vampire.
- **Cloned the vampire** to study its strengths/weaknesses before the money ran out.
- Turned to the **druids of this grove** for daylight spells. *Ferrin chimes in: "He's talking about us!"*
- Late notes: started **playing fast and loose with safety**; many tests went sideways.
- Alludes to a **final experiment** that would let him kill the vampire for good.
- **Companions are never named** in the journal.
- Last entries: concerned for his own mind, but trusted **companions to keep him in check and stable**.

## Loot consolidation

### Viltevar — Archive merge

- Ferrin shows [[Viltevar]] how to **fold his awakened spellbook into the [[Abracadabrus and Astromancy Archive|archive]]**.
  - Original spellbook is **absorbed** — gone as a separate item.
  - Archive now carries the **awakened grimoire benefit** (spellcasting focus + bonus to wizard spell attack rolls and save DCs, scaled by the absorbed book's rarity).
  - All of [[Viltevar]]'s transcribed spells are now in the archive.
- Viltevar tried to feed in **[[Udious]]'s spellbook — it would not absorb.** Udious's book remains a separate physical spellbook in Viltevar's pack.
- **New spells** (going forward) get written **onto the sphere's surface** at the normal book-transcription cost in time + materials. Including, eventually, Udious's spells if Viltevar wants them in the archive.
- Net: one focus, one container, awakened-grimoire bonus, all of Viltevar's own spells — plus a physical backup book of Udious's spells still to mine.
