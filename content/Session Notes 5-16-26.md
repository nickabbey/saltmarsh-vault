## Picking up from last session

Cliffhanger: Door 3 opened, [[Viltevar]] strode forward with a "Ahoy, mate!" Trap triggered. A wall dropped behind him, cutting the party in two — [[Viltevar]] and [[Shade]] inside the hallway with the stone-like sentinel; [[Amos Crane|Amos]], [[Kaldor]], and [[Vyth]] sealed out. (Vyth was a step or two behind Viltevar and got clipped by the closing force but hopped clear.)

## The fight — stone sentinel in the hallway

### Round 1 — sealed in

- **[[Shade]]** caught the force-shove from the closing wall, recovered with a roll into a run, drew his **new heavy crossbow**, and fired three shots — two hits. Ended his turn halfway down the hall.
- **Stone sentinel** advanced; used a magical command and forced [[Shade]] prone.
- **[[Vyth]]** felt the force from being near the door, dodged clear, then dropped a **Bless** on himself, [[Shade]], and [[Viltevar]].
- **[[Viltevar]]** attempted to **Dispel Magic** on the closed wall. Nothing happened. Passively read a **transmutation aura** on the former door — dispel ineffective. (Best guess in hindsight: *Stone Shape* — instantaneous, nothing to dispel.)
- **[[Kaldor]]** cast **Thunderwave** at the wall from the outside, blasting a hole large enough to rush through.

### Round 2 — the rest of the party crosses the threshold

- Crossing into the room **triggers a tidal-force effect**. [[Amos Crane|Amos]] was shoved violently to his knees on entry. [[Kaldor]] thought better of rushing through.
- **[[Shade]]** rolled through another tidal pulse, attacked from prone, **pierced the sentinel's armor**, then stood with momentum to try to tank it.
- **[[Amos Crane|Amos]]** crossed in, hurled his **javelin** — it transformed mid-air into a **bolt of lightning** that struck the sentinel. He **blinked** to its side and swung — glancing blow off the armor.
- The **sentinel** stepped over [[Shade]] (Amos got an OA on the way), reached [[Viltevar]], and connected a haymaker — knocked [[Viltevar]] prone. **[[Vyth]] reaction:** interposed divine forces, padded the damage. The sentinel then poured **necrotic energy into a vicious bite that healed it.** (Self-bolstering / regeneration — important.)

### Round 3 — Viltevar pinned, Haste deployed

- At start of [[Viltevar]]'s turn: tidal force slammed him back to the ground for damage. [[Vyth]] interposed again; landed even more this time.
- Standing was like moving through molasses (the tidal effect is also slowing).
- **[[Viltevar]]** stood and cast **Haste on [[Amos Crane|Amos]]**.
- **[[Kaldor]]** moved in, made his Strength save against the tidal force, cast **Moonbeam** on the sentinel.
- **[[Shade]]** circled, struck at exposed inner workings — perfect dagger strike, **zero damage.** Confirmed: **immune to nonmagical damage.**
- **[[Amos Crane|Amos]]** (hasted, three attacks): first hit was a **Smite**; the radiant damage **lit the creature up** and from then on **all attacks against it had advantage**. (Functionally Faerie Fire / similar — Amos's smite or javelin effect.)
- The sentinel stepped out of the moonbeam → **OAs from everyone, all with advantage**. As it circled and began powering up an area attack, **[[Kaldor]] connected with a spell attack and dropped it.** Down, twitching.

### Round 4 — it's not over

- Tidal forces in the room are **still active** even with the sentinel down.
- [[Viltevar]] knocked down again at start of turn, took damage, held concentration on Haste. **Misty Step** out of the room.
- From the hallway, [[Viltevar]] reads the magical auras on the destroyed wall: looks like **Stone Shape** — the wall had been pulled together like stretched dough.
- The group inside moved toward the chest to look for the trap's off switch.

## The chest puzzle

- [[Kaldor]] noticed an **indentation in the shape of a book** and a **slit for a blade** on top of the chest.
- [[Shade]] considered his new runed sword/rapier, approached the chest (the tidal force did not affect him on the way), and **slid the blade into the slit**.
- [[Viltevar]] felt a pull on his pack — something inside trying to come back into the room. [[Shade]] called: *"Throw the story book to me!"* [[Viltevar]] muttered *"aww, but I love that book"* then aloud: *"if you think it will work, I'll get it to you."*
- Meanwhile **the sentinel reassembled itself and stood back up** — confirming the self-repair theory. It attacked [[Vyth]]; the slow effect was still on Vyth; the sentinel hit hard. **[[Vyth]] responded with massive necromantic damage and put it down again.** Witnessing the regen, [[Viltevar]] confirmed: **it heals up while we're in the room.**
- [[Amos Crane|Amos]] spotted **runes on the ceiling**, threw his javelin at them — no effect. Called this out. [[Viltevar]]: *"on it!"*
- [[Viltevar]] shrugged his pack off to test whether the pull would let it be dragged. Pack hit the ground — and immediately understood: **it was a teleportation effect, not telekinesis.** The story book warped from his pack to next to [[Shade]] at the chest.
- [[Viltevar]] then **animated his spellbook** and projected it into the **dead center of the room, halfway to the ceiling.** Used it to scry the room and **cast Chill Touch** on the sentinel to keep regen suppressed while the puzzle resolved.
- [[Kaldor]] grabbed both books from the pack — **[[Udious]]'s spellbook** and the **story book** — held them up to [[Shade]] for comparison against the indentation.
- **Story book + rapier matched.** Both absorbed into the chest.
- The chest **began to levitate** and float around the room. Runes on the ceiling faded. The chest **tipped forward and opened.**

## Loot — the chest contents

The chest floated to the center of the room and settled. Lid floated up to the ceiling; front fell forward. Inside: a pile of **gold and gems**, with an **orb** on top.

### The orb — *Abracadabrus and Astromancy Archive*

- Surface marked with **swirls and lines like ley lines on a globe.**
- Radiates **abjuration and divination** magic.
- The party somehow *knows* the title: **Abracadabrus and Astromancy Archive.**
- [[Viltevar]] tried to open it — **locked.** Resists attempts. Warrants further study. Stowed in pack.

### The coinage

- Gold and gems. Each coin is **stamped with an ancient pre-Kingdom [[Keoland History|Keoland]] mark** — predates the modern kingdom.

### Plan

- **Repair this room and use it as the adventuring-loot vault.**
- Inn (Building 1 at [[Crabber's Cove]]) still gets first priority for restoration funds.
- When we left the room, the chest closed itself behind us.

## Short rest in the hexagonal room

- Banter. [[Viltevar]] makes another attempt at the orb — still resists.
- Group decides Room 4 (south door, still locked at start of last session) is next.

## Room 4 — the grove

- [[Shade]] takes point, draws his weapon, pushes through the door — which was unblocked.
- Room interior is **a lush grove**. Living trees, real bark, real leaves (confirmed by [[Viltevar]] leaning against one).
- A **film or membrane** between the doorway and the grove proper. The party stepped through.
- **[[Amos Crane|Amos]] had a sensory experience on entry** — a look like he'd wandered into his grandmother's house with fresh cookies baking, an autumn breeze through his hair, before snapping back.
- [[Shade]] questioned aloud whether the whole room was an illusion. Physical contact says no — at least not a simple one.
- [[Shade]] spotted **a pattern in the grass** that looks like a **teleportation gate destination marker.**
- Animal life rich and varied. [[Vyth]] noted he'd **never seen some of these creatures** before.
- [[Amos Crane|Amos]] spoke (Speak with Animals?) with a **two-tailed squirrel**, who said: *"You are in the Grove of Obad-Hai."*
- [[Viltevar]] was **enthralled** by the grove.
- [[Shade]] tried to find the exit. A voice asked: *"Leaving so soon?"*
- A **middle-aged halfling with a bullfrog on his shoulder** appeared. Druidic demeanor and garb. Introduced himself as **[[Ferrin Kastilar]]**. *"I'm glad to have visitors to my grove."* (Bullfrog's name: **Lorys**.)

**Session ended here.**
