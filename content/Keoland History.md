The **Kingdom of Keoland** — the realm [[Saltmarsh]] belongs to, on whose remote southern coast the village sits. ([[Viltevar]] idiosyncratically calls it "Keoghland.")

For generations Keoland was a formidable military power; its cavalry and knights pushed the kingdom's borders north, west, and east, while the southern coast stayed a neglected backwater where piracy flourished — conditions that let the [[The Sea Princes|Hold of the Sea Princes]] rise and raid the coast. Saltmarsh suffered heavily, and local hatred of the Sea Princes runs deep. In time those northern victories reversed into defeats that pushed Keoland back to its old borders. **King Kimbertos Skotti** then made peace with his northern neighbors, raised a navy, and checked the Sea Princes. The crown's new decree: put down piracy, secure the sea lanes, and cultivate trade — so Saltmarsh is entering a new phase, with royal agents wanting to expand its port and a king-sanctioned dwarven crew arriving to mine the nearby hills.

## What the [[Saltmarsh Renegades]] Know

- The pre-Kingdom Keoish coinage found in the [[Crabber's Cove]] tower hoard ([[Session Notes 5-16-26|5-16-26]]) predates the modern kingdom — a dating clue worth pursuing carefully (provenance matters).
- [[Viltevar]] holds a standing grievance against the crown: he believes it impoverished his family by taxing without granting sufficient incomes — the root of both his mercantile-empire ambitions and his disdain for the king-sanctioned mining interests (the "avaricious whales" of Keoghland).
