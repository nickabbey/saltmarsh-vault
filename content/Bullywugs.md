Swamp-dwelling frog folk who occupy territory in the coastal marshes north of [[Saltmarsh]], near the [[Lizardfolk Camp]]. They are hostile to the lizardfolk and were one of the reasons the lizardfolk were displaced from this land originally.

## Encounter

The party encountered a bullywug warband while travelling through the marsh to reach the [[Lizardfolk Queen]]. Their leader, a self-important frog king mounted on a giant frog, ambushed the group with about a dozen bullywugs. Two of the lizardfolk hatchling guides were killed in the fight, including the only one who spoke Common.

The party routed the bullywugs, but the frog king escaped; narrowly avoiding being snatched off his mount by the party's druid (who had wild shaped into a giant frog himself).

## Status

The frog king is still at large in the marshes. The bullywugs remain a nuisance faction in the region, hostile to both the lizardfolk and anyone allied with them.
