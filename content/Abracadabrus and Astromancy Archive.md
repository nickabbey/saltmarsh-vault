# Abracadabrus and Astromancy Archive

The party's name for the orb recovered from the hexagonal chest in [[Crabber's Cove]] ([[Session Notes 5-16-26|5-16-26]]). Unlocked by [[Ferrin Kastilar]] in [[Session Notes 5-23-26|5-23-26]].

Held by [[Viltevar]]. **Attuned, wizard-only.**

## Apparent origin
Underlying mechanics match the canonical **Astromancy Archive** (Tasha's Cauldron of Everything). The DM has reskinned the form (presents as a sphere with ley-line swirls rather than the canonical brass disc) and added a custom **journal** from the previous owner. The "Abracadabrus" prefix appears to be homebrew naming.

## Mechanics (canonical Astromancy Archive)

> Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement by a wizard)

Brass disc of articulated, concentric rings. **Bonus action** to unfold into an armillary sphere (or back into a disc).

When found, contains these spells, which count as **wizard spells for you while attuned**:
- Augury
- Divination
- Find the Path
- Foresight
- Locate Creature
- Locate Object

Functions as a **spellbook**, with spells encoded on the rings.

Holding it = can use as a **spellcasting focus** for wizard spells.

### Charges
- **3 charges**, regains **1d3** expended charges daily at dawn.

### Charge uses
- **Swap a prepared spell (1 charge, 1 minute):** spend 1 minute studying it, expend 1 charge to replace one prepared wizard spell with a different spell from the archive. The new spell must be a **divination** school spell.
- **Reaction d4 (1 charge):** when a creature you can see within 30 ft makes an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, use your reaction to expend 1 charge and force the creature to roll a d4 and apply it as a **bonus or penalty** (your choice) to the original roll. Decide after seeing the roll, before effects apply.

## Homebrew additions

- **Embedded journal** from [[Udious]] (full name *Udious Odiuous*). Contains the backstory of his hunt for the vampire [[Xoltec]], the founding of his tower, his collaboration with the Sea Grove druids, and his late-life experiments. See [[Session Notes 5-23-26]] for the full read.
- **Visual / interface flavor:** [[Viltevar]] perceives the contents as a heads-up display in his mind, with spells aligned to **celestial spheres**.
- **Locked when found** — needed [[Ferrin Kastilar]]'s instruction to open.
- **Spellbook absorption (DM ruling, [[Session Notes 5-23-26|5-23-26]]):** with Ferrin's guidance, Viltevar's **awakened grimoire was absorbed** into the archive. The archive now confers the **awakened grimoire benefit** (spellcasting focus + bonus to wizard spell attack rolls and save DCs based on the absorbed book's rarity) and contains **all of Viltevar's own transcribed spells**.
- **[[Udious]]'s spellbook would not absorb** and remains a separate physical book in Viltevar's pack.
- **New spells:** written **onto the sphere's surface** at the normal book-transcription cost in time + materials — same procedure as scribing to a spellbook. Includes any of Udious's spells Viltevar later wants in the archive.
